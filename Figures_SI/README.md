# Figures
Scripts in this directory are used to create the supplementary figures included in the SI.

## Directories
* addFeW - figures including Fe, W Volmer-Tafel pathways (S16-S19)
* coverage - plots of adsorbate-adsorbate interactions (S14-S15)
* DRC - Degree of Rate Control plots (S32-S35)
* Hey_taf_selectivity - selectivty plot of Tafel/Heyrovsky rates (S30)
* OHads - plot comparing Volmer barrier with and without OH adsorption on Ni 
* electronic_correlations - plots relating energetics and electronic descriptors (S11-S12)
* revision - S20

| Figure | File | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| S9 | `figure_electronic_descriptors_activation_energies.plot` | Activation free energies vs electronic descriptors
| S10 | `hbe_electronic_descriptors.plot` | H binding free energies vs electronic descriptors
| S21 | `linear_activity_hbe.plot` | Exchange current vs H binding free energy (linear correlation)
| S22 | `correlation_OHj0.plot` | Exchange current vs OH binding free energy
| S24 | `pzc_htop_relation.plot` | Relation between PZC and H (top) binding free energies
| S31 | `figure_polarization_curves.plot` | Polarization curves
